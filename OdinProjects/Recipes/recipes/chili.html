Chili

Description

Chili is a meal that is considered strong and balanced. It mainly contained meat and beans, these two ingredients are dense enough to fill a stomach for hours.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 (46 fluid ounce) can tomato juice
  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 and 1/2 cups chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chili powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.

  3. Drain and discard grease.

  4. Transfer browned beef into a large pot over medium heat; stir in tomato juice, tomato sauce, kidney beans, pinto beans, onions, bell pepper, chili powder, cumin, salt, sugar, oregano, ground black pepper, and cayenne pepper.

  5. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 and 1/2 hours.
