Chicken katsu accompanied with salad and rice

Description

Chicken katsu is a famous japanese fried chicken meal. It is usually paired with rice or noodle. In this recipe it is paired with rice and salad for a more balanced meal. The crunchyness of the chicken mixed with the softness of the rice make a marvelous combination.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings: pepper and salt are enough
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.

  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.

  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
